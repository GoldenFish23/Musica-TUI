[ OVERVIEW ]
Musica is designed for maximum efficiency on 8GB RAM systems. By using a TUI 
(Text User Interface), it saves critical resources for your primary tasks 
like Gaming, Coding, CAD Designing or AI Modeling.

On an 8GB system, every megabyte counts. Musica provides:
